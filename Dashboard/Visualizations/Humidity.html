Humidity

It’s shown in the chart that the maximum relative humidity stays constant across latitudes at about 100%, while the lowest relative humidity trends up latitude getting close to 0. which means there is higher chance to have higher humidity near the equator. It echoes to the fact that temperature increases when approaches the equator, and therefore will accelerate evaporation and increases humidity.
